Direct rate, OTA bundle, "deal" package with breakfast — different totals, different lengths of stay, no easy way to compare them. This tool strips out the noise and shows the true per-night value of any set of rates, from both sides of the booking.

  • Guest View — what you actually pay per night, after crediting perks like breakfast or late checkout
  • Revenue Manager View — what the property actually nets per night, after channel commission and perk cost
  • Free, no login, works on mobile — built as an applied revenue-strategy project
